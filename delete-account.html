Account & Data Deletion

Request permanent deletion of your GameKit Pro account and all associated data.

How to Request Deletion

You can delete your account on GameKit Pro and go to the SideBar > Subscriptions section and select Delete Account

For further assistance, please send an email to:

Support Email
omn9dev.app.sup@gmail.com

Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request – GameKit Pro

Information to Include

Please include the following details in your email so we can process your request:

  • Your account email
  • Your User ID (if known)
  • A short confirmation that you want your data deleted

Processing Time

We will confirm your request and notify you once the deletion process is complete. Most requests are processed within a few business days.
